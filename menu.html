Wines

_

The Reds

Chambourcin 2013 (Estate) Limited: Has been 9 years since we have bottled this hybrid. Best we have ever produced. Nice white pepper notes in the nose and the finish with mid black cherry undertones. 14.99
Cabernet Franc 2013 (Grand River Valley): Bold cherry and raspberry flavors mingle with spicy black pepper notes and balanced amount of French Oak. This wine was aged in half new and half used French Oak for ten months. 24.00
Cosmo (American) Super Tuscan Red Blend: Cosmo is our family's Super Tuscan Style house red. Medium-bodied with aromas of dark chocolate and dried red fruit. Soft and silky in the mouth with tempered flavors of candied cherry. Sangiovese 75%, Merlot 25% 19.99
Rodavi (American): Intense flavors of raspberries and blackcurrant are supported by firm tannins and toasty oak. Deeply satisfying. Merlot 50%, Cabernet Franc 50% 19.99
Pinot Noir 2012 Single Barrel Reserve: Seductive aromas of spring strawberry and black tea. Notes of red licorice and tobacco leaf in the mouth. Delicate, with escalating mouthfeel and texture and good tannin structure. Long finish of earth and strawberry preserves. 29.99
CASK 1014 (Grand River Valley) Limited: Aged in French Oak. This continual red blend of Cabernet Franc and Cabernet Sauvignon has bold, black cherry and currant flavors with a hint of black pepper notes. Nice tannin structure will allow this wine to age beautifully. 29.99
Cabernet Sauvignon 2012 (Grand River Valley) Reserve: Aged in French Oak. Aromas and flavor of ripe blackcurrant and plum. This rich, full-bodied wine is well structured and will age nicely. 34.99
Naso Rosso Semi-Sweet Red: Luscious and grapey, structured and rich at the same time. Concord + Cabernet Franc 9.99

The Whites

Josephine 2012 (Grand River Valley): Luscious aroma of Key Lime zest. Flavors of crushed red apple and mandarin. Lively and enjoyable acid-tartness and a round, persistent finish-with snap! Viognier 50%, Chardonnay 50% 16.99
Pinot Grigio 2013 (Grand River Valley): Vibrant acidity on the front of the palate opens up to creamy grapefruit and tropical pineapple flavors. A touch of sweetness carries the fruit flavors to every corner of your mouth. 16.99
Riesling Off-Dry 2013 (Grand River Valley): Snappy crisp aromas of green apple and juicy pear. Soft flavors of white peach and zesty lemon-lime with a firm acid backbone. A long finish with only the slightest hint of sweetness. 16.99
Illuminata 2013 (Grand River Valley): Off-dry with aromas of white peach, white nectarine and roses. Flavors of lemon-lime and green apple with a nice acidity. Long finish with a slight hint of sweetness. 35% Riesling, 35% Gewürztraminer, 30% Muscat Blanc 16.99
Connie's Blush Semi-Sweet Rosé: Flavors of ripe summertime strawberry and juicy candied cherry. Pink Catawba + Vidal 9.99
Naso Bianco Semi-Sweet White: A floral and slightly honeyed nose. Flavors of lemonade and ripe apple. Vidal + Niagara + Riesling 9.99

Dessert Wines

Sweet Genevieve Ice Wine (Vidal): Made from our Estate-grown Vidal grapes, left to hang through the winter and harvested frozen. Richly textured, with exotic pineapple, mango and Meyer lemon flavors. Balanced acidity makes for an exquisite and mouth-watering finish. *GRV Awarded Double Gold Medal 34.99
Simply Mad Habanero Ice Wine: Made from Vidal Ice Wine, infused with Habanero peppers; just enough to finish with a KICK! 34.99
